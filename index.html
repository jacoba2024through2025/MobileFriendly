John Carmack: A Pioneer In Computer Game Design

John Carmack was the lead designer for iconic game titles like Doom, Quake, Wolfenstein. While working at the company he cofounded, Id Software, he programmed the Quake engine for Wolfenstein in a month. The Quake engine for Doom took him around a year to make. To this day, John is a pioneer in game design, and he paved the way for many different first-person shooter franchises like Half Life, Counter-Strike, Call of Duty.
